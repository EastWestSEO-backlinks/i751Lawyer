What is the I-751 Petition?

The I-751 petition is an essential form filed by individuals who hold conditional permanent resident status. Conditional status is typically granted to those who have been married to a U.S. citizen for less than two years at the time their residency was approved. This two-year conditional period is meant to ensure the marriage is bona fide and not entered into solely for immigration purposes.

To remove these conditions and become a full permanent resident, you must file Form I-751 during the 90-day window before your conditional green card expires. Failure to file in time can result in the loss of your residency status, making it crucial to complete the process correctly and on time.
